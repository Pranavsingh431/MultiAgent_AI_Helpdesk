Employees receive 20 sick leaves per year, plus 10 casual leaves. Annual vacation entitlement is 25 days for employees with 1-5 years of service, and 30 days for 5+ years. Maternity leave is 6 months paid, paternity leave is 2 weeks paid. Emergency leave can be granted by immediate supervisor approval. All leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 48 hours in advance, except for emergency situations. Unused casual leave cannot be carried forward, but vacation days can be carried forward up to 5 days to the next year. Public holidays are separate from personal leave quotas.
