Employees can request keyboards, mice, laptops, monitors, and other IT equipment through Admin team via the IT portal. Standard laptop configurations: MacBook Pro 14" or Dell Latitude 5520 based on role requirements. Desktop accessories include wireless keyboard, mouse, and 24" monitor. Requests are processed within 3-5 business days. All hardware remains company property and must be returned upon employment termination. Damage or theft must be reported immediately to IT security. Personal use of company equipment is prohibited. Software installation requires IT approval. Hardware refresh cycle is 3 years for laptops, 5 years for desktops. Warranty support available through IT helpdesk.
