Salaries are credited on the last working day of every month by 5 PM local time. Direct deposit is mandatory for all employees. Pay stubs are available in the employee portal 48 hours before payday. Annual salary reviews occur in January with performance-based increments. Overtime is paid at 1.5x rate for non-exempt employees after 40 hours per week. Bonus payments are processed in March and September. Tax deductions follow federal and state guidelines. For salary discrepancies, contact payroll@company.com within 5 business days. Banking details can be updated through HR with 30-day notice period.
