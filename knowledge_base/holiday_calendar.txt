The annual holiday list is published on the HR portal by December 1st for the following year. Standard holidays include New Year's Day, Martin Luther King Day, Presidents Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving, and Christmas Day. Floating holidays: 2 personal days that can be used for religious or cultural observances. Office closure dates are announced 60 days in advance. Holiday pay is provided for full-time employees who have completed 90 days of employment. Part-time employees receive prorated holiday pay. If a holiday falls on weekend, it is observed on the adjacent Friday or Monday. Holiday schedules may vary by location and are subject to local regulations.
