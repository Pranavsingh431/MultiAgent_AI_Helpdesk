To access the company VPN, use vpn.company.com with your employee ID and corporate password. VPN access is available 24/7 for all employees working remotely. For initial setup, download the Cisco AnyConnect client from the IT portal. If you experience connection issues, ensure your firewall allows VPN traffic on ports 443 and 1723. For authentication problems, contact IT support at it-support@company.com or call extension 1234. VPN logs are monitored for security compliance.
