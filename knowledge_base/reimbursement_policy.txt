Employees can claim work-related reimbursements via finance portal at finance.company.com. Eligible expenses include travel, accommodation, meals during business trips, training courses, and office supplies. Upload original receipts within 30 days of expense. Meal reimbursements are capped at $50 per day domestic, $75 international. Taxi/rideshare receipts required for transportation claims. Alcohol expenses are not reimbursable. Approval workflow: Manager → Finance → Processing within 7 business days. Personal credit card statements are not acceptable - only merchant receipts. For amounts over $500, prior approval required.
